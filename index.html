About Me

My area of interests is Structural health monitoring, Damage analysis, Computational mechanics, Finite element methods, Bridge engineering, Probability and Reliability analysis.

After having 4 years of industry experience as Structural Design Engineer from 2010-2014, I completed my Ph.D from N.I.T.K. Surathakl form 2014 July – 2017 December, I am here in NIE from January 2018, serving as Assistant Professor in Department of Civil Engineering, Lab In-charge of PG Dynamics lab (2019 June – Till date). Actively involved in NBA and NAAC related works. I have given couple of expert talks on technical aspects in other institutes and organisations and contributed as conference reviewer & also active Doctoral committee member.

I have been guiding UG and PG students from past 2018 – till date and I have been giving exposure to students to attend various renowned International conference in NIT’S and IIT’S, please go through my complete profile for all details.

For the benefit of students, I have been building websites for the subject I taught, which consists of lecture plan, lecture note, presentations, Video lectures quizzes, question bank for UG and PG level. For more information please explore my faculty profile.
